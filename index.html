Flagship Project & Competition Entry

Submission to the IAEA "Shaping African Futures" Competition

Atoms for Ethiopian Resilience

This project is a comprehensive proposal for using peaceful nuclear applications to solve critical challenges in Ethiopian agriculture. It outlines a 4-part solution leveraging nuclear technology to ensure food security, reduce post-harvest losses, and empower local farmers.

  • Mutation Breeding: To develop drought-resilient, high-yield crops.
  • Isotopic Analysis: For precision water and fertilizer management.
  • Sterile Insect Technique (SIT): For eco-friendly pest control.
  • Food Irradiation: To reduce waste and improve food safety.
